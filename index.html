We are a group of developers & designers from Europe and the United States, curious about the emerging African tech hubs we keep hearing about.

We decided to fill the “blank canvas” ourselves, by visiting some tech communities in the Eastern African Community in September/October 2013. We’ll share our impressions and experiences with the global community by creating a video documentary.

Our goal is to kick of a continuous exchange between the new tech communities emerging in Africa and the rest of the global tech community.

trip

When
September / October 2013, 4 weeks
Costs
~ $ 2,000, excluding arrival / departure. We are looking for sponsors
Participants
~ 12
Locations
Nairobi, Kampala, Kigali and Dar es Salaam

events

Organizing Hackathons is one of the ways we want to engage with the African Tech communities. The first is coming up on Dec 1st/2nd, as part of Random Hack Of Kindness taking place in San Francisco, Berlin, Kampala and Kenya.

Stay tuned!
