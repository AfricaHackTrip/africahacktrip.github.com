Public Website for our 2012 Africa Hack Trip

What the hack?

Originally inspired by the article "From Kenya to Madagascar: The African tech-hub boom" We decided to organize a trip through Africa's Hacker Spaces. Due 2013. And yes, you're invited. Get involved.

Download PressKit

Objective

We want to explore and connect with the emerging African tech communities, get to know their creatives, learn about their projects, challenges and potentials. We'll document our experiences to "fill the blank canvas", replace stereotypes with human stories and spark interest to connect and collaborate with new members of our global tech communities.

Current State

When? June / July 2013
How Long? 4 weeks
Costs? ~ $ 2,000, excluding arrival / departure. Yes, we are looking for sponsors.
Locations? Nairobi, Kampala, Kigali and Dar es Salaam
Participants? 10 + 2 local creatives

Starting in June 2013, we will visit the tech communities of Nairobi, Kampala, Kigali and Dar es Salaam and stay at each place for around a week. We'll visit with 10 creatives from Europe and the United States, with two additional places reserved for local participants.

During our journey, we will collaborate with local developers to work on open source projects, to learn from each other and to exchange experiences. We also plan to organize an event at each location we will visit.

As an outcome, we will create a video documentation showcasing the communities, their coworking spaces and their developers.
