Public Website for our 2012 Africa Hack Trip

What the hack?

Originally inspired by the article "From Kenya to Madagascar: The African tech-hub boom" We decided to organize a trip through Africa's Hacker Spaces. Due 2013. And yes, you're invited. Get involved.

Objective

We want to explore and connect with the emerging African tech communities, get to know their creatives, learn about their projects, challenges and potentials. We'll document our experiences to "fill the blank canvas", replace stereotypes with human stories and spark interest to connect and collaborate with new members of our global tech communities.

Current State

When? Semptember / Ocober 2013
How Long? 3 - 4 weeks
Costs? ~ $ 1,000, excluding arrival / departure. Yes, we are looking for sponsors.
How Many Locations? 4 - 7. We want to stay at least 3 days per location.
How Many Participants? 8 - 12

We'll start and finish the trip in Nairobi, Kenya. This is the first trip of its kind and we want to make sure both that it will be a success and that it will be only the first of its kind. So when you can't participate this time, get on the waiting list for the next trip in 2014.

We are currently fine tuning our Objective. Before end of September, we want to crowd source a map of potential locations, with as much information about the hubs, hosts and the local communities. In October we want to decide on the final locations we want to visit and start the concrete planning. From November will start searching for sponsors.
